1. Click on the "Edit" icon (pencil) on the post page.
2. Change the "Title" field to <new_title>.
3. Change the "Body Content" field to <new_body>.

Expected results:
1. Success message "Post successfully updated." appears.
2. The page displays the new title <new_title> and the new body <new_body>.
3. The changes are saved and persist after refreshing the page.

1. Delete this post.
2. Return to the Home Page.
